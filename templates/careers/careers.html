Working at Samson

Working in Facilities Management can be rewarding and very challenging, every task is different and we need to be adaptable at all times to provide the right service at the right times and often at short notice, if you like being kept on your toes, this is the right industry for you.

Facilites management

Facilities management covers a wide range of services. Samson made their start in security but expanded in to reception, cleaning, landscaping and other unique services related to the specific needs of the client on the day. We need multiskilled and committed staff to help us adapt to the challenges that are presented to us as an FM organisation.

Adaptability

Here at Samson we do the best we can to push those who have the drive and the talent up through the ranks, as a company who are always on the lookout for talent and new growth opportunities, we give those willing to help us the best chance possible to do so.

Training

Opportunities

Why do training?

Receiving quality training from a company like Samson gives you the qualifications necessary to stand out in your specialisation. Learning leads to better job opportunities and the best chance of advancement through the industry.

What can we offer ?

We provide courses in the major areas of Facilites Management. Security and every facet of it, primarily focused on one of the most challenging environments to operate within, Health care. We also train professional cleaners, receptionists and engineers.

When is the right time to train ?

We suggest receiving the training for your desired role before applying for jobs, this will uniquely position you above competition to secure roles and be recommended for advancement through management.
