Corporate Social Responsibility

Since moving in to our new HQ in Wavertree we have began opening the space to the local community. With cooking classes for children on Wednesdays and Fridays and a number of planned community and charitable initiatives such as our partnership with the armed forces covenant, we aim to have the gold award by the end of 2022. We allow veterans charities to use our space to meet with ex-forces members to help them transition in to work and recieve financial, welfare and housing support. We engage our community of staff to commit to charitable causes and bring the team together to overcome challanges for charity.
