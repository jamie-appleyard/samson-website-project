Who we are

Combining state-of-the-art technology with fully qualified staff members of high calibre, Samson Security Fire & Facilities is Merseyside’s only privately owned monitoring station, which offers an extensive range of security and facilities management services.

Renowned for delivering a level of protection that is second-to-none, we pride ourselves on our tailored and flexible approach to ensure that all of your security needs are covered. Whether you want to safeguard your business or home, we are able to provide you with a professional integrated security service to cover all of your requirements.

To ensure that you receive only the best customer satisfaction, we carry out every job to an unsurpassed standard. All of our staff members are heavily regulated, and therefore undergo frequent regulation checks and training programmes. By doing so this allows us to maintain our brand reputation, in addition to our Security Industry Authority (SIA) approved contractor status. In order to cover all areas of security care, we provide an extensive portfolio of forward-thinking solutions.

From domestic clients to local independents and big name brands we are proud to work with such a varied array of people and businesses. As well as being responsible for the NHS’ National Framework, we have provided a range of services to recognised and esteemed brands including Clarke Energy, Toyota, Premier League, Avon Estates and Liverpool City Council.
