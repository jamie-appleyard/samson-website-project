Alarm Receiving Centre

All of our CCTV, intruder alarm and fire alarm monitoring facilities are carried out in accordance of the legal requirement of BS EN ISO 9001:2015, and BS 5979: 2007, and strive to go above and beyond that of traditional on-site surveillance methods. Confined securely within the 14” thick walls of ‘The Vaults’, our diverse surveillance monitoring systems are linked to the emergency services (police, fire and ambulance) to enable immediate response following all alarm activations.

RVRC

Ideal for any commercial or public sector property, our Remote Video Response Centre uses only the most cutting-edge digital technology and transmission systems. Our highly skilled operators receive a signal when an intruder breaches the perimeter of your property, verify the intrusion by accessing your CCTV system, we can then issue verbal warnings via on site speaker systems, send out a patrol or send out the emergency services immediately.
